Under our assumption, the matrix \(A-\lambda_{0}I\) has rank \((n-1)\) and \((A-\lambda_{0}I)\bv=\bc\) has a solution iff \(\bc\cdot \bx_{0}=0\text{.}\) Thus (4.5.8) has a solution when \((s\bx_{0}+\bw)\cdot \bx_{0}=0\text{,}\) which determines \(s\) uniquely: \(s=- \bw\cdot \bx_{0}\text{.}\) (4.5.9), together with (4.5.8), then determine \(\bv\) uniquely.
