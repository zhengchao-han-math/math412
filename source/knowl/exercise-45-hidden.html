Let \(f^{+}(x)=\max (f(x), 0), f^{-}=\max(-f(x), 0)\) denote the positive and negative parts of \(f(x)\) respectively. If \(f\) is integrable on \(C\text{,}\) is it true that \(f^{+}\) and \(f^{-}\) are also integrable on \(C\text{?}\) If both \(f^{+}\) and \(f^{-}\) are integrable on \(C\text{,}\) is it true that \(f\) is integrable on \(C\text{?}\)
