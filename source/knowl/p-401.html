Suppose that \(f_{1}, f_{2}\) are Riemann integrable on \(R\text{,}\) and \(c_{1}, c_{2}\) are constants, then \(c_{1}f_{1}+c_{2}f_{2}\) is also Riemann integrable on \(R\text{,}\) and
\begin{equation} \int_{R}\left( c_{1}f_{1}+c_{2}f_{2}\right) = c_{1} \int_{R} f_{1} + c_{2} \int_{R} f_{2}.\tag{5.1.1} \end{equation}
